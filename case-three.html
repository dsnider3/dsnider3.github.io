Olivecrest

Visual / UX Design

Olivecrest is a nonprofit organization that transforms the lives of at-risk children through the healing power of family. At the end of 2016, Olivecrest approached Grizzard Communications Group to design a new website to not only reflect their new visual identity but also to offer more comprehensive services and information about each individual chapter of the organization. Their impact has grown tremendously over the last decade but the need continues to grow.

Scope

For my contribution to this project, the creative brief tasked me with the following:

  • Establish a user experience wireframe of all website pages
  • Determine how users switch between chapter / location specific content
  • Create mockups for the homepage and one additional content page

Solution & Results

2015 marked noticeable trends with device adoption and how donors chose to give based on the devices they own. The following year, generatiional shifts in donor behavior and the adoption of new giving platforms resulted in a significant trend that became a major highlight in the 2016 edition of Donorgraphics. Following changes in Washington, new trends in 2017 emerged as post-election responses by younger generations led to larger donations in support of at-risk institutions and liberties.
