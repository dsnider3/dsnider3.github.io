Welcome

Spatial Operations is a showcase of creative pursuits by multi-disciplinary designer and front-end developer Daniel Snider. His work spans the fields of web development, graphic design, animation, architecture, and urban design. Spatial Operations as a name is derived from the unique design exercises that define "space" across various mediums. My creative work gives meaning to an environment, a mission, an identity, a message and/or a product.
