Biography

Daniel Snider is a multi-disciplinary designer and front-end developer. He previously worked as an Art Director at One & All, an Omnicom agency that specializes in fundraising for nonprofit organizations. In that role he was responsible for designing print, digital, and web assets that enhanced the ability of One & All's clients to raise charitable funds in support of their respective missions. He is also a specialist in infographic design. Formerly educated in the field of architecture, Daniel secured his Bachelor of Science in Architecture at the University of Cincinnati, attended Georgia Institute of Technology for a masters in the same field before pivoting his career towards graphic design and web development.

Daniel is an animal lover, community advocate, cyclist and a well-traveled individual with a love for cities and public spaces.
