Biography

Daniel Snider is a multi-disciplinary designer and front-end developer working at One & All, an Omnicom agency that specializes in fundraising for nonprofit organizations. In his role as an Art Director, he is responsible for print, digital, and web assets that enhance the ability of its clients to raise charitable funds to support their respective missions. He is also a specialist in infographic design. Formerly educated in the field of architecture, Daniel secured his Bachelor of Science in Architecture at the University of Cincinnati, attended Georgia Institute of Technology for a masters in the same field before pivoting his career towards graphic design and web development.

Daniel is an animal lover, community advocate, cyclist and a well-traveled individual with a love for cities and public spaces.
